Mechanical engineers design mechanical systems and devices like engines, machines, and HVAC systems. They work across industries such as automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing.

  • Robotics
  • Aerospace Engineering

Electrical Engineering

Electrical engineers work with electrical systems and electronics. They are involved in designing circuits, power systems, and communications technology.

  • Power Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering
  • Control Systems
  • Telecommunications

Software Engineering

Software engineers develop computer programs, apps, and systems. This field involves coding, system design, and solving technical challenges in software development.

  • Web Development
  • Mobile App Development
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Systems Engineering

Chemical Engineering

Chemical engineers apply principles of chemistry and physics to design processes that produce chemicals, fuel, food, and pharmaceuticals. They also focus on sustainability and safety.

  • Process Engineering
  • Biochemical Engineering
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Materials Engineering
